# Helpshift Engineering Manager Handbook

**Your comprehensive interview preparation repository for the Engineering Manager — Developer Productivity & Platform Engineering role at Helpshift.**

---

## Repository Purpose

This handbook is a structured, self-contained study guide designed to prepare you for every stage of the Engineering Manager interview process at Helpshift. It covers company research, technical deep-dives, management fundamentals, leadership storytelling, and mock interview practice.

---

## Study Roadmap

The recommended preparation order follows a logical progression:

```
Phase 1: Company & Role Context (1 week)
    ↓
Phase 2: Technical Preparation (2–3 weeks)
    ↓
Phase 3: Management Preparation (2–3 weeks)
    ↓
Phase 4: Mock Interviews & Revision (1 week)
```

---

## Recommended Preparation Order

| Order | Section | Duration | Focus |
|-------|---------|----------|-------|
| 1 | `01-company-profile/` | Week 1 | Understand the company, product, culture, and role fit |
| 2 | `02-technical-preparation/` | Weeks 2–4 | Deep-dive into Platform Engineering, K8s, CI/CD, SRE, System Design |
| 3 | `03-management-preparation/` | Weeks 5–7 | Management fundamentals, leadership stories, behavioural prep |
| 4 | `04-mock-interviews/` | Week 8 | Practice rounds, cheatsheet revision, final preparation |

---

## Folder Descriptions

### `01-company-profile/`
Research and notes on Helpshift's business, products, customers, competitors, engineering culture, and why you're a strong fit.

### `02-technical-preparation/`
Technical content covering Developer Productivity, Platform Engineering, DORA metrics, CI/CD, Kubernetes/EKS, GitOps, AWS, SRE, Observability, DevSecOps, system design scenarios, and a technical question bank.

### `03-management-preparation/`
Engineering management fundamentals, leadership & stakeholder management, roadmaps & strategy, behavioural interview preparation, leadership stories, a first-90-days plan, and a management question bank.

### `04-mock-interviews/`
Structured mock interview templates, a hiring manager round guide, and a final revision cheatsheet.

### `assets/`
Supporting materials including diagrams, images, and personal notes.

---

## How to Use This Repository

1. **Fork or clone** this repository.
2. Follow the **Recommended Preparation Order** above.
3. Work through each markdown file sequentially within each section.
4. Fill in your own research, experiences, and STAR stories as you go.
5. Use the **question banks** to self-quiz and track readiness.
6. Revisit the **cheatsheet** in `04-mock-interviews/` before each interview round.
