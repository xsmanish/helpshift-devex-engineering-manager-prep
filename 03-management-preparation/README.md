# 03 — Management Preparation

## What Is Covered

This section covers the management and leadership aspects of the Engineering Manager interview. Topics include engineering management fundamentals, leadership & stakeholder management, roadmaps & strategy, behavioural interview preparation using the STAR method, leadership stories, a first-90-days plan, and a curated management question bank.

## Learning Objectives

- Articulate your engineering management philosophy and approach.
- Demonstrate leadership and stakeholder management capabilities.
- Explain how you drive strategy, roadmaps, and execution across teams.
- Prepare compelling behavioural answers using the STAR framework.
- Develop and rehearse your own leadership stories.
- Create a concrete, actionable first-90-days plan.
- Practice with a comprehensive management question bank.
