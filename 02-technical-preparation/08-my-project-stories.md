# Project 1 – NextGen CLI Platform

## Executive Summary

Led the development of a self-service Internal Developer Platform capability through a custom NextGen CLI built using OCLIF and TypeScript. The platform standardized CI/CD onboarding, automated repository and pipeline generation, and reduced dependency on platform engineers, enabling application teams to onboard consistently while improving developer experience and engineering velocity.

---

## Business Problem

Application onboarding relied heavily on platform engineering teams.

Every new application required:

* Manual repository setup
* Pipeline creation
* Configuration management
* Environment integration
* Platform team intervention

This created bottlenecks and slowed engineering delivery.

---

## Engineering Manager Talking Points

* Treated Platform Engineering as a product
* Established golden-path onboarding
* Reduced cognitive load for developers
* Increased self-service capabilities
* Improved developer experience
* Improved onboarding consistency

---

## Executive Sound Bite

> We transformed platform onboarding from a ticket-driven process into a self-service developer experience by creating a CLI-based platform abstraction layer that standardized engineering workflows and accelerated delivery.

---

## Keywords

* Internal Developer Platform
* Developer Productivity
* Self-Service Engineering
* Golden Paths
* Developer Experience
* Platform Product Mindset
* Platform Abstractions
* Engineering Velocity
* Workflow Standardization
