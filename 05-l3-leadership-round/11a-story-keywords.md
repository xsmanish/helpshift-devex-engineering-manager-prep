# People Management Story Bank – Quick Reference

## 1. Code of Conduct Issue

**Theme:** High Performer, Poor Behavior

**Situation**

* Senior engineer
* Strong technical contributor
* Interrupting others
* Dismissing ideas
* Junior engineers stopped participating

**Actions**

* Gather facts
* SBI feedback
* Private discussion
* Set expectations
* Coaching plan
* Follow-up feedback

**Result**

* Behavior improved
* Better collaboration
* Increased participation

**Leadership Message**

* Performance ≠ excuse for behavior
* Protect culture

---

## 2. Improving Team Visibility

**Theme:** Recognition & Influence

**Situation**

* Platform team invisible
* Only noticed during incidents
* Low morale

**Actions**

* Monthly reviews
* Impact dashboards
* Cost savings
* Reliability metrics
* Engineers present work
* Leadership updates

**Result**

* Increased recognition
* Better morale
* More executive support

**Leadership Message**

* Create visibility intentionally

---

## 3. Non-Performer Improvement

**Theme:** Coaching

**Situation**

* Missed commitments
* Quality issues
* Stakeholder complaints

**Actions**

* Root cause analysis
* 1:1 discussion
* Found context switching
* Reduced workload
* Weekly checkpoints
* Clear ownership

**Result**

* Performance recovered
* Stakeholder trust restored

**Leadership Message**

* Fix causes, not symptoms

---

## 4. Giving Negative Feedback

**Theme:** Difficult Conversations

**Situation**

* Strong engineer
* Poor stakeholder interactions

**Actions**

* Specific examples
* Focus on impact
* No personal criticism
* Communication coaching
* Growth plan

**Result**

* Better influence
* Improved relationships

**Leadership Message**

* Behavior + Impact

---

## 5. Balancing Strong & Weak Performers

**Theme:** Team Capability Management

**Situation**

* Seniors overloaded
* Juniors underutilized

**Actions**

* Work complexity mapping
* Stretch assignments
* Mentorship
* Growth plans
* Delegated ownership

**Result**

* Balanced workload
* Faster growth
* Less burnout

**Leadership Message**

* Build capability, not heroes

---

## 6. Promotion Rejection

**Situation**

**Actions**

* Review criteria
* Gap analysis
* Development plan

**Result**

**Leadership Message**

## 10. Delegation Lesson

**Theme:** Leadership Growth

# 15-Second Interview Triggers

### Underperformance

Root Cause → Coaching → Support → Accountability → Improvement

### Negative Feedback

Facts → SBI → Impact → Expectations → Follow-up

### Conflict

Listen → Align Goals → Data → Decision → Commit

### Retention

Understand Motivation → Growth Path → Opportunity → Recognition

### Promotion

Criteria → Gaps → Development Plan → Evidence

### Conduct Issue

Facts → Private Discussion → Expectations → Coaching → Monitor

### Morale Issue

Listen → Identify Pain Points → Reduce Friction → Recognize Wins

### Team Building

Right Person → Right Work → Coaching → Growth

### Leadership Philosophy

Trust → Clarity → Ownership → Growth → Results

### Manager vs IC

IC solves problems
Manager builds people who solve problems
