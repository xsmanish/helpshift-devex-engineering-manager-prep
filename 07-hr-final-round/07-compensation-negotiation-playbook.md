# Negotiation Strategy

## Step 1

Express excitement.

Example:

> I have genuinely enjoyed the conversations throughout the interview process and I am excited about the opportunity to join Helpshift.

---

## Step 2

Acknowledge the role.

Example:

> The role aligns extremely well with my background in platform engineering, reliability, engineering leadership, and organizational effectiveness.

---

## Step 3

Discuss compensation professionally.

Example:

> Since my current compensation includes a performance-linked component and Helpshift follows a fixed compensation model, I am evaluating the opportunity from a total compensation perspective.

---

## Step 4

Present expectation.

Example:

> Based on my experience and current compensation structure, I am targeting a package in the ₹68–70 LPA range.

---

## Step 5

Remain flexible.

Example:

> I am certainly open to discussing the overall package and would like to understand the complete compensation and benefits structure before finalizing expectations.

---

# If Helpshift Offers ₹62–63 LPA

Do Not Reject Immediately.

Recommended Response:

> Thank you for sharing the offer details. I am genuinely excited about the opportunity. I was hoping for a package slightly closer to the ₹66–68 LPA range considering my current compensation structure and the absence of a variable component. Is there flexibility to explore this further?
