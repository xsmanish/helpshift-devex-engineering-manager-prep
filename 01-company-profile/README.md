# 01 — Company Profile

## What Is Covered

This section focuses on researching and documenting Helpshift's business, product, engineering culture, and the specific Engineering Manager — Developer Productivity & Platform Engineering role. Understanding the company deeply is the foundation for tailoring your interview responses.

## Learning Objectives

- Know Helpshift's core business and value proposition.
- Understand the product portfolio, target customers, and competitive landscape.
- Internalise the engineering culture, team structure, and developer productivity initiatives.
- Articulate a clear, compelling answer to: *"Why Helpshift and why this role?"*

## Suggested Reading Order

1. `01-company-overview.md` — Business model, history, mission, funding.
2. `02-products-customers-competitors.md` — Products, customer segments, key competitors.
3. `03-engineering-culture-developer-productivity.md` — Engineering org, culture, DevEx initiatives.
4. `04-why-helpshift-and-role-fit.md` — Personal alignment, role fit, motivation statement.
